# 🍽️ FoodBreeze AI

FoodBreeze AI is an AI-powered food redistribution platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting surplus food providers with NGOs, volunteers, and people in need.

## 🚀 Problem Statement
Millions of kilograms of edible food are wasted every day while many people struggle with hunger. Existing donation systems are often slow, manual, and lack intelligent logistics.

## 💡 Solution
FoodBreeze AI uses Artificial Intelligence to:
- Detect food type and freshness from uploaded images.
- Estimate remaining shelf life.
- Match donations with the nearest NGO or volunteer.
- Optimize delivery routes.
- Track donation status in real time.
- Generate analytics on rescued food and environmental impact.
